Subprocessors

Last updated: 08 November 2025

We work with a small set of specialist vendors that help us deliver, host, and support the BrambleAI platform. Each subprocessor only receives the minimum amount of data needed to provide their service, and every contract includes the required data protection commitments.

Supabase Inc.

Vercel Inc.

Hosting and execution of frontend assets and serverless (Edge) functions.

Render Inc.

Hosting of API services.

Mapbox

Mapping, geocoding, and routing services.

Google Workspace

Business email, file storage, and internal collaboration.

Notion Labs Inc.

Internal project management and requirements tracking.
